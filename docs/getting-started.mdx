# Getting started

## What you get

A temperature string is a single cable with temperature sensors at known
points along its length, plus a microcontroller at one end that reads all
the sensors and logs or transmits the data.

## Who it's for

- Lake and pond researchers measuring thermoclines
- Farmers and growers tracking soil temperature profiles
- Greenhouse and controlled-environment operators
- Process engineers monitoring tanks and columns
- Hobbyists and educators who want a real, open sensor platform

## What you'll need

- The OpenString PCB (ordered from a fab house — see [Ordering](./ordering.mdx))
- The parts listed in the BOM
- Soldering equipment
- A computer with the Arduino IDE installed
- Sealing / potting supplies appropriate to your deployment

## Build path

1. **Order** the PCB and parts — [Ordering](./ordering.mdx)
2. **Assemble** — [Assembly](./assembly.mdx)
3. **Flash** firmware — [Firmware](./firmware.mdx)
4. **Deploy** and read data
