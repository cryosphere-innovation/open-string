## Can I sell OpenString-based products?

Yes. All three licenses allow commercial use. You must preserve
attribution and — if you modify the hardware design, firmware, or docs —
release the modifications under the same license.

## Can I keep my modifications private if I only use them internally?

Generally yes. The share-alike obligation triggers when you *distribute*
a derivative work. Internal use alone does not require publication.
See the full license texts for exact terms.
