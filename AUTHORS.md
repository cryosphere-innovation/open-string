OpenString is released by **The OpenString Authors** — the community of
people who have contributed hardware, firmware, documentation, or
testing to the project. Copyright in the project's files is held
collectively by that community.

This file is the canonical list.

## How to add yourself

Open a pull request that edits this file. Small additions ("fixed a
typo in docs") are welcome too — the goal of this list is to make the
humans behind the project visible, not to gate-keep.
